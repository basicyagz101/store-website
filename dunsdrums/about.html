About Dun's Drums

From our Founder, Joshua Dun,

"When i was younger I wanted to create art. I always knew that I wanted to make some kind of art that was my own. When I got older, I started working at a music store and realized who I wanted to be. I found the drums and I loved them. The problem? I couldn't afford them, I was still just a teenager so I had to ask my parents for a drum set, and when they saw the price tag, they told me it wouldn't work out. Eventually I got a pretty cheap dum set that served me well, but it was still a bit flimsy, and all the components were some of the cheapest on the market, so it didn't sound the best. Now, as the drummer of one of the most famous bands in the the world, Twenty One Pilots, I can make sure kids and adults alike can find affordable drum sets that are made with good materials, can be used for nearly a lifetime, and look just as good as they sound."

*Not an actual quote*
